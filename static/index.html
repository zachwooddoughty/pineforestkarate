Welcome

There are many reasons to be interested in karate, and many reasons why our dojo is the place to start your journey. Here are some of the reasons why our members choose to train with us:

  • Great location in Florence: just 6 minutes from Northampton and 23 minutes from Amherst.
  • Our beautiful studio space.
  • An accomodating schedule with classes six days a week.
  • Collaboration with the international Ueshiro Shorin-Ryu Karate USA organization.
  • Unlimited attendance for a low monthly fee.
